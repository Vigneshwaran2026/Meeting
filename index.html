Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our meeting and collaboration platform.

What is an online meeting platform?

Our platform allows teams to host HD video meetings, share screens, and collaborate in real-time from anywhere.

Do participants need to install software?

No installation is required. Participants can join meetings directly through their browser using a secure meeting link.

How many participants can join a meeting?

Our meeting platform supports small team meetings as well as large online conferences with hundreds of participants.

Is the meeting platform secure?

Yes. We use end-to-end encryption and secure meeting rooms to ensure your conversations and data remain private.

Do you have career opportunities?

Yes. We are always looking for talented developers, designers, and support specialists to join our growing team.

Do you offer remote work?

Yes, our company supports remote and hybrid work to enable flexible collaboration for our employees.

Do you offer flexible pricing plans?

Yes. We offer flexible plans for individuals, teams, and large organizations depending on meeting usage.

Is there a free plan available?

Yes. We provide a free plan for small teams with basic meeting and collaboration features.
