FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Meeting Platform

How do I start an online meeting?

Starting a meeting is simple. Click the "Start Meeting" button, generate a meeting link, and share it with your participants. They can join instantly using their browser or mobile device.

Do participants need to install any software?

No installation is required. Participants can join meetings directly through a secure link using their web browser on desktop or mobile devices.

Is the meeting platform secure?

Yes. Our platform uses end-to-end encryption, secure meeting rooms, and participant controls to ensure your meetings and conversations remain private and protected.

Can I record my meetings?

Yes. You can record meetings and save them securely in the cloud. Recorded sessions can be shared with team members or accessed later for reference and collaboration.
